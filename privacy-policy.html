Privacy Policy

Who We Are

Neuron Digital Solutions S.L. (hereinafter "Neuron") is the data controller responsible for the personal data collected through this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and applicable national legislation.

What Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data when you use our website or contact us:

  • Identification data: name, surname, email address, and phone number provided via our contact form.
  • Navigation data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site, collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communication data: the content of messages you send us through the contact form or by email.

How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To manage the contractual relationship with our clients.
  • To send commercial communications, only with your prior consent.
  • To improve our website and services through anonymous statistical analysis.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Legal Basis for Processing

The legal bases for processing your data are:

  • Consent: when you voluntarily provide your data through the contact form or subscribe to our communications.
  • Contractual necessity: when processing is required to fulfil a service agreement with you.
  • Legitimate interest: for fraud prevention, network security, and improving our services.
  • Legal obligation: when required by applicable law.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Contact form data is kept for a maximum of two years unless a business relationship is established.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten") where applicable.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@neuron.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g. hosting, analytics) under strict data processing agreements that ensure your data is protected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
